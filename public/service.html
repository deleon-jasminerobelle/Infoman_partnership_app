Subscription Plan

Small Scale ₱519 per tree

Ideal for 10–199 trees, fully planted and maintained

1. Small scale farming is ideal for those who want to plant with purpose — not just for profit.

Think of it as your way of giving back to the earth and supporting Filipino farmers. For legacy builders, climate champions, and community contributors.

Here's what you get:

  • You’ll help plant real coffee trees in the highlands of Sitio Dueg, Tarlac.
  • Though there’s no guaranteed financial return, you will receive:
    • A small share of your tree’s harvest when it's ready
    • A quarterly box of goods from Farm to Cup Philippines (fruits, vegetables, seasonal produce) for 1 year for free.

This plan supports climate action, rural livelihoods, and reforestation. It’s not just farming — it’s planting legacy, one tree at a time.

Large Scale ₱407 per tree (min 200)

Bulk planting with intercropping options

2. Large scale farming is designed for those ready to grow a real agricultural asset — starting with a minimum of 200 coffee trees. For serious impact investors, companies, and organizations planting at scale.

Here’s what makes it powerful:

  • You receive a harvest share from your coffee tree portfolio
  • While coffee takes 3–4 years to mature, we maximize your investment by offering:
    • Intercropping options — grow crops and/or livestock between your coffee trees (faster ROI, may take only months while waiting for your harvest on your coffee.)

This creates short-term yield while waiting for your long-term coffee harvest. Your entire farming operation is managed by Aniya & Farm to Cup Philippines — from planting to maintenance.

Build your own farm legacy, earn from harvests, and help regenerate the land — all at once.
